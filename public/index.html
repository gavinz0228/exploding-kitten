How to Play

Objective: Avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten card. If you draw one, you explode and are out of the game unless you have a Defuse card to defuse it.

Setup:

  • Each player is dealt a hand of cards.
  • The deck is shuffled and placed in the center.
  • Players take turns drawing cards from the deck.

Turn Structure:

  • On your turn, you can play as many cards as you like from your hand, or none at all.
  • After playing cards, you must draw a card from the deck to end your turn.

Card Types:

  • Exploding Kitten: If drawn, you explode and are out of the game unless you have a Defuse card.
  • Defuse: Use to defuse an Exploding Kitten and place it back in the deck.
  • Nope: Stop any action except Exploding Kitten or Defuse.
  • Attack: End your turn without drawing a card and force the next player to take two turns.
  • Skip: End your turn without drawing a card.
  • Favor: Force another player to give you a card.
  • Shuffle: Shuffle the deck.
  • See the Future: Look at the top three cards of the deck.
  • Cat Cards: Collect matching pairs to steal a random card from another player.

Winning the Game: The last player remaining who has not exploded wins the game.
