How to Play

Objective: Be the last player standing by avoiding Exploding Kittens!

  • Each player starts with 4 cards + 1 Defuse card
  • On your turn, play action cards (optional) then draw a card
  • If you draw an Exploding Kitten, use a Defuse card or you're out!
  • Use action cards strategically to survive and eliminate opponents

Card Types:

  • Skip: End turn without drawing
  • Attack: Next player takes 2 turns
  • See Future: Look at top 3 cards
  • Shuffle: Shuffle the deck
  • Favor: Force another player to give you a card
  • Cat Cards: Play pairs to steal cards from opponents
